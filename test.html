Enecovery’s Patented Thermo Catalytic Conversion (TCC)

Enecovery’s TCC is a heterogeneous catalytic process engineered for safe, efficient molecular reformation of complex hydrocarbon feedstocks. It reforms oil shale, tar sands, plastic, rubber, oil sludge and municipal waste into sulphur-free fuel derivatives and co-products, with minimal emissions and zero residue.

Core Features

  • Complete conversion to sulphur-free hydrocarbon fuels
  • Operates up to 450°C at near-atmospheric pressure
  • Produces high-calorific LPG (~9000 Cal/kg)
  • Delivers consistent output quality for industry use

Safety & Reliability

  • Replaceable Multi-Functional Cartridge (MFC) to prevent vapour/pressure build-up
  • Two-way product discharge for safe handling
  • Designed for low maintenance and long operational runs
Process Flow (summary)
+

Feedstock → Thermal Catalysis → Molecular Reformation → Distillation/Refining → Fuel & Co-products (LPG, Carbon, Water). No segregation required for mixed waste streams.

Environmental & Emissions Controls
+

Integrated gas-cleaning, condensate recovery and emission monitoring to ensure minimal atmospheric impact and regulatory compliance.

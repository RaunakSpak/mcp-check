Mr. Kush Singh

Managing Director

Sector Head – Power, CESC Limited (RP–Sanjiv Goenka Group) B.Tech – Mechanical Engineering, BIT Mesra | IIM Calcutta | 39+ Years of Transformative Leadership Kush Singh is a power sector architect with over 39 years of transformative leadership experience, shaping the future of energy across India and international markets. Currently serving as Sector Head – Power at CESC Limited, he oversees 10,000+ MW of power assets and operations serving 3.7 million+ consumers across diversified geographies. A graduate in Mechanical Engineering from BIT Mesra and alumnus of IIM Calcutta, Kush’s distinguished career spans leading organizations including NTPC, Adani, Essar, and CESC. He has been instrumental in India’s first undersea power link to Singapore, globally ranked renewable projects, and large-scale transitions from thermal dominance to clean energy leadership.

His global footprint extends across China, Japan, South Korea, Canada, Indonesia, Venezuela, Iran, and the UAE, driving multi-billion-dollar projects in nuclear power, coal gasification, renewable integration, lithium refining, and hydrogen-based energy systems. As a strategic technology leader, he has pioneered initiatives in: • Waste-to-Energy and Green Hydrogen Production • Solar, Wind, and Energy Storage Integration • Coal Gasification and SMR Nuclear Technologies • Lithium Refining, Battery Manufacturing, and Recycling • Fuel-to-Power-to-Chemical Value Chains Recognized for his thought leadership and policy advocacy, Kush is a Co-Chair of the ICC National Expert Committee on Energy, a keynote speaker at global energy forums, and a regular contributor to NDTV Profit, Indian Infrastructure, and Power Line Magazine. A firm believer in the energy transition imperative, his vision is rooted in rigor, innovation, and long-term sustainability — advancing India’s journey toward a decarbonized, technology-driven power ecosystem.

Advisor – Strategy

Vikas Chaturvedi is a financial strategist and advisor to leading multinational corporations across India and Europe. Himanshu Niranjani

Advisor- IT

Himanshu Niranjani is a seasoned technology leader with over 25 years of global experience across Microsoft, Amazon, LinkedIn, Meta, and Verizon. As the founder of Agile Catalyst and Be Human Capital, he has driven innovation at the intersection of advanced technology, business transformation, and human-centered design. At Aarava, he is the architect of the company’s AI-enabled digital mining ecosystem, developing intelligent platforms that enhance operational efficiency, predictive decision-making, and safety across mining operations. His leadership blends strategic vision, deep technical expertise, and an entrepreneurial mindset—positioning Aarava at the forefront of digital transformation in the global mining sector.

P. L. Sharma

Vikram Kaushal

Sachin Pawar

Harshal Rajpara

Dr. Shyama Prasad Manna

Advisor – Technology and Business Development
